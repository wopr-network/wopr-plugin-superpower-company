# Run a Company from Discord

Your company lives in Discord now.

---

Every channel becomes a department. Every department gets a dedicated employee who actually does the work. They wake on schedule, check in, read messages, draft emails, track tasks, and coordinate with each other.

You don't manage an AI. You manage a team. The #marketing channel has a marketing lead. The #engineering channel has an engineering lead. They talk to each other in #general when something needs cross-functional work.

This is what a $5/month workforce looks like.

## What it can do

- Assign a dedicated employee bot to each Discord channel
- Employees wake on schedule and check in with their department
- Cross-channel coordination when work spans departments
- Draft and send emails on behalf of departments
- Track tasks and deadlines per department
- Status reports to #general on demand

## What gets installed

- **Cron** -- scheduled wake-ups and check-ins
- **Gmail** -- department email drafting and sending
- **Discord** -- channel-as-department mapping
- **Skills** -- task tracking and coordination
